About us

Smart Cup
On the go.

Tiered to make stop for cold and hot drinks... We Presents you with the ultimate solution of SMART CUP... Which can keep your cold drinks cold and hot drinks hot... While you drive..

Advantages
- Connect to cigarette-lighter directly (12V DC 36W). General Size for most car.
- Cools to 15°C below ambient temperature (Coolest at 5°C). Heats to 55°C.
- Use full aluminum material for good heat conduction.
- Portable size (165*106*106 mm), easy carry for travel, enjoy your drink anytime.
- Cools 15°C below ambient temperature in about 30min, maximum cooling capacity at 5ºC, Heat to 50ºC in about 30 min
